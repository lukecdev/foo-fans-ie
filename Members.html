Band Members

Dave Grohl

Lead Singer, Guitarist, Songwriter

He is the founder of the band. Dave is the lead singer, guitarist, and principal songwriter. Before forming Foo Fighters, he was the drummer of the world-renowned band Nirvana from 1990 to 1994. Grohl has been inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame as part of Nirvana in 2014 and as a member of Foo Fighters in 2021. Along with Foo Fighters, Grohl is the drummer and co-founder of the rock supergroup Them Crooked Vultures and has recorded and toured with Queens of the Stone Age.

Nathan Mendel

Bass guitarist

Mendal is the second longest-serving member of the band and appeared on nine of the band's studio albums. A former member of the indie rock band Sunny Day Real Estate before joining Foo Fighters.
